Ingredients
- 1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta 
- 1 teaspoon olive oil 
- 2 cloves garlic, minced 
- 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
- Chicken Breasts Boneless Skinless 
- 1 lb  crushed red pepper flakes to taste 
- 1/3 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips 
- 1/2 cup pesto sauce Add all ingredients to list 

Directions
- Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. 
- Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
- Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. 
- Saute garlic until tender, then stir in chicken. 
- Season with red pepper flakes. 
- Cook until chicken is golden, and cooked through
- In a large bowl, combine pasta, chicken, sun-dried tomatoes and pesto. Toss to coat evenly.
